FLAME — 20 ANIMATION VARIANTS

Same chubby blob + particles. Only the flame's motion differs.
01Slow Breath
Just gentle scale 1 → 1.05 over 2.4s. Calm, candle on still air.
02Still Air
Barely moves. 4.5s cycle, ±1deg skew, minimal scale. Meditative.
03Wood Dance
Medium tempo (1.5s) with skew + scale. Real wood-fire vibe.
04Strong Gust
Heavy slow ±7° sway over 3s. Outdoors fire in the wind.
05Stepped Flicker
Frantic 0.42s with steps(6) — twitchy not smooth. Stepped digital feel.
06JS Noise (RAF)
requestAnimationFrame applies smooth Perlin-style random transforms. Truly organic.
07Asymmetric Breath
Left and right pump on different schedules. Slightly off-balance, alive.
083-Speed Independent
Translate (0.5s) + rotate (1.7s) + scale (3.3s). Never syncs — never repeats.
09Packed Irregular
9 irregular keyframes (11%, 23%, 37%, ...) crammed into 2.6s. Random-feeling flicker.
10Full Lifecycle
Whole blob shrinks to 0.55, grows back to 1.06, dies again. Mimics burn cycle.
11Turbulence Warp
SVG feTurbulence + animated noise. Body shape itself ripples organically.
12Path Morph
Same blob shape continuously morphs between 3 subtle path variants. Liquid.
13Jitter + Breath
Fast 0.3s jitter (translate) layered on slow 2.4s breath (scale). Two motions at once.
14JS Random Pulse
JS triggers random surge events at irregular 0.6-2.4s intervals. Unpredictable.
15Periodic Surge
Calm 90% of the time, then strong scale-up + brightness flash every 4s. Combustion.
16Alternating Lean
Leans hard left → centre stretch → leans hard right → repeat. 4-stop sway.
17Hyper-Noisy
11 irregular keyframes over 3.7s with combined skew/scale/translate. Flickers like real fire.
18Spring Bounce
Elastic cubic-bezier easing — bouncy overshoot at peaks. Cartoon-ish bounce.
19Slow Sway + Crackle
3s smooth sway underneath + 0.2s crackle jitter on top. Two timescales mixed.
20Combo + JS
2 stacked CSS animations (slow rotate+scale + fast translate) AND JS perturbation. Maximum life.
